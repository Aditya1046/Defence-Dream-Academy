About Academy

At Defence Dream Academy, we are more than just a training institute; we are a launchpad for aspiring defenders. Located in delhi, we are passionately committed to shaping determined young individuals into disciplined and capable leaders ready to serve the nation. Our academy provides comprehensive training programs designed to equip candidates with the knowledge, skills, and mental fortitude required to excel in the rigorous selection processes of various defence forces. 1 We understand that joining the armed forces is a calling, a dream nurtured with dedication and perseverance. That's why our experienced instructors, many of whom are veterans themselves, provide personalized guidance and mentorship every step of the way
